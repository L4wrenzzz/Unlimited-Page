Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering, placing an order, or filling out forms. This includes your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment details. We also automatically collect non-personal data such as your IP address and browsing behavior to improve our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to process and fulfill your orders, manage your account, communicate with you regarding your purchases or inquiries, and to personalize and improve your shopping experience on Unlimited Page.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal data. We only share your information with trusted third-party service providers (such as payment gateways and shipping couriers) strictly for the purpose of fulfilling our services to you.

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. All sensitive payment data is processed securely and is never permanently stored on our direct servers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time. You can manage most of this data directly within your Account Profile, or you can contact our support team for further assistance.
